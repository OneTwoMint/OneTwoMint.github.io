Design

Here's a few highlights of my work that's more about the design than anything else- Logos, posters, fliers, even product visualisation and advertisements. Most of the work here is done in Adobe Illustrator.

Micro Pad Promotional Images

This project is a series of promotional images for the fictional "Micro Pad," a bluetooth game controller that's roughly the same size and shape as a smartphone. It's a product idea my brother came up with, and if he ever gets the right tools for it, perhaps it'll become a reality. But until then, I was able to use Blender, an open source 3D modeling software, to showcase what it might look like. I'm also proficient in Autodesk Maya, though Blender is my preferred 3D modeling program.

Eclipse Wine Label

I was asked to design a custom wine label to commemorate a family gathering around the recent total solar eclipse, which coincided with a family member's birthday quite nicely. The design went through a lot of revisions, but everyone was delighted by the end result, and I had a lot of fun with it.
