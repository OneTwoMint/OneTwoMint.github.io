3D Modeling

My selection of 3D work is limited, due to the time it takes and not having had many opportunities to make portfolio quality work before; However, I'm still proud of it, and eager to expand my portfolio with new projects if you need my talents. I'm trained in Autodesk Maya, and proficient in Blender.

"Micro Pad" Renders

You can find a little more information about this project on my Design page, but the short version is, my brother had an idea for a small, portable controller! It's just an idea, so I needed to make a mockup of what it'd look like. Here's the renders I made for those advertisements!

Pastel the Cat Vs. Some Robots

This video was the culmination of my education in Autodesk Maya! A simple little animation with characters made in the style of a low-poly 3D video game. The models were technically made in Blender, but the materials and animation were all done in Maya, and I was instructed in how to model and rig in Maya as well. I just prefer working in Blender for modeling. The quality of it as a video leaves something to be desired, there's some timing and audio issues and the last few seconds render twice- But otherwise it's a project I'm fond of.
