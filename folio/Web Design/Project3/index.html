Becoming Characters, Assuming Identities

[Intro, talk about Based On A True Story and trying to come up with a theme to connect four of the pieces I really liked. In each, a person or place becomes a character, a part of a fiction. Rather than being about the changing of identities, the goal is to use an assumed identity to examine a fiction. This site is meant to talk about these works from this perspective, and talk about who these artists are.]
