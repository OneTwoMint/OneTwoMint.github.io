The Author, Mint

Me! I'm a nonbinary demigirl, and my pronouns are she/her or they/them. If those words are strange to you, don't worry about it, just talk about me like you would talk about any woman and you'll be doing fine. I like video games mainly, like Sonic the Hedgehog, Hollow Knight, Metroid, and of course, Cave Story, the game Cave Tale is based on.

I've always wanted to make comics, and still do of course, though I don't currently have any comic-making projects as I'm just trying to get by through college. I was born in Minnesota somewhere, though I couldn't tell you anything about it as I mainly grew up in State College, Pennsylvania. It's a nice college town, and surprisingly urban for a place surrounded on all sides by 3 hours of farmland to drive through. I lived in Provo, Utah for a few years, (an experience I wouldn't recommend,) and now live in Chicago, Illinois. I like Chicago. Tall buildings get me excited, and there's plenty here.
